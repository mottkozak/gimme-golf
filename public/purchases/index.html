This page explains how paid features are billed and managed for Gimme Golf.

Current Status

In-app purchases and subscriptions are coming soon and are not yet active.

Where Purchases Are Processed

Purchases are processed by your platform store provider:

  • Apple App Store (iOS)
  • Google Play (Android)

We do not directly process card payments for in-app purchases.

Subscriptions

  • Subscription terms (price, trial, renewal cycle) are shown before you subscribe.
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled before the renewal date.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your platform account settings.

Refunds

Refund eligibility is determined by Apple or Google under their own policies. To request a refund, use the relevant store support workflow.

Price Changes

We may change pricing for future billing periods where permitted. Your platform store will notify you when required.

Restoring Purchases

If your purchase is not recognized after reinstalling or switching devices, use your store account restore flow and ensure you are signed in with the same account used for the purchase.
