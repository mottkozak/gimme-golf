EXTRA COMMON CARDS

These should stay very doable.

C19. Fair Miss

Miss the fairway, but stay in bounds and avoid hazards.
Reward: +1

C20. Safe Green

Miss the green, but leave yourself a chip or putt from a makeable position.
Reward: +1

C21. Advance and Survive

No full shot travels backward or sideways more than forward.
Reward: +1

C22. No Three Off the Tee

Do not hit out of bounds off the tee.
Reward: +1

C23. Lag Master

Your longest putt of the hole finishes within 4 feet.
Reward: +1

C24. Good Number

Make net par relative to your normal level for the hole.
Reward: +1

C25. Fairway or Fringe

Finish in either the fairway, fringe, or green with your approach sequence.
Reward: +1

C26. Trouble Avoided

Do not hit water, bunker, trees, or OB on the hole.
Reward: +1

C27. Tap-In Finish

Make your final putt from 3 feet or less.
Reward: +1

C28. Calm Hole

No penalty strokes and no putt longer than three attempts.
Reward: +1

C29. Green-Side Safety

If you miss the green, miss it green-side rather than short-sided or hazard-side.
Reward: +1

C30. Tee Box Competent

Hit your tee shot in play and advance it at least a solid distance.
Reward: +1

EXTRA SKILL CARDS

These should reward legitimately strong play without requiring miracles.

S19. Dart Board

Hit any approach inside 15 feet.
Reward: +2

S20. Fringe Genius

Miss the green but get down in two shots or fewer from just off the surface.
Reward: +2

S21. Clutch Two-Putt

Reach the green from 40+ feet away and still avoid a 3-putt.
Reward: +2

S22. Par 4 Precision

On a par 4, hit fairway and make par or better.
Reward: +2

S23. Pin High

Finish your approach pin-high, regardless of left/right miss.
Reward: +2

S24. Long Iron Life

Use a long iron or hybrid on approach and still hit the green or fringe.
Reward: +2

S25. Scramble Touch

Miss the green, chip inside 6 feet, then make the putt.
Reward: +2

S26. Big Putt Energy

Make a putt between 8 and 15 feet.
Reward: +2

S27. Fairway Pressure

Hit the fairway after another player already completed a drive-related mission.
Reward: +2

S28. Course Management

Take the safe play when danger is in play, then still make par or bogey.
Reward: +2

S29. Drive for Show

Finish with one of the two longest drives in the group and still make bogey or better.
Reward: +2

S30. Green Window

Hit the correct tier/section of the green as agreed by the group before the shot.
Reward: +2

R27. Bounce-Back Birdie

R28. Zero Panic

R29. Kill Shot

R30. No Regrets

EXTRA CHAOS CARDS

This is where the little gremlins start wearing ties.

X19. Double Trouble

If two or more players fail their missions, all successful players gain +1 extra.
Public

X20. Rich Get Richer

Current game leader earns +1 if they complete any mission this hole.
Public

X21. Robin Hood

Current game leader loses 1 point if they fail; last place gains 1 if they succeed.
Public

X22. Par 3 Panic

On a par 3, anyone missing the green cannot earn mission points.
Public

X23. The Collector

Any player who completes their mission and makes par or better steals 1 point from the current leader.
Public

X24. Heat Check

If the same player succeeds on three holes in a row, they gain +2 extra here.
Public

X25. Freeze Out

The previous hole’s winner cannot earn bonus points from Chaos this hole.
Public

X26. Mercy Rule

Any player in last place gets +1 just for completing a Common card this hole.
Public

X27. Group Project

If everyone in the group makes bogey or better, everyone gets +1.
Public

X28. Disaster Dividend

If any player cards triple bogey or worse, all other players get +2.
Public

X29. Clutch Multiplier

Any made putt over 12 feet is worth +2 instead of +1 bonus.
Public

X30. Fairway Tax

Any player missing the fairway on a par 4 or 5 loses 1 game point unless their mission specifically involves recovery.
Public
