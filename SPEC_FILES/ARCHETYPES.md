🧬 CORE ARCHETYPE SET (clean + reusable)

These should cover ~80% of rounds:

🎯 Performance-Based
	•	🎯 The Sniper → high success rate, low mistakes
	•	🧊 The Closer → strong final holes
	•	🔥 The Heater → streaks (hot/cold runs)
	•	🧱 The Wall → avoids big numbers
	•	📉 The Rollercoaster → volatile (high highs, low lows)

⸻

🎲 Strategy / Playstyle
	•	🎲 The Gambler → lots of risk cards
	•	🧠 The Strategist → leans safe, high % plays
	•	⚖️ The Balanced One → even mix of card types
	•	🕶️ The Opportunist → capitalizes on chaos/props
	•	🪤 The Trap Setter → benefits from others failing

⸻

🎭 Personality / Vibes (these are share gold)
	•	🤡 The Chaos Agent → triggers chaos constantly
	•	🗣️ The Instigator → influences others’ outcomes (style cards, group effects)
	•	🎤 The Broadcaster → leans into style/play-by-play energy
	•	😈 The Villain → steals points, ruins others’ rounds
	•	🧃 The Vibes Guy → not winning, but carrying morale

⸻

⸻

🎲 Card Behavior Driven
	•	📦 The Collector → completes tons of missions
	•	🚫 The Bricklayer → fails a lot of missions
	•	🎁 The Freeloader → gains from chaos without doing much
	•	🧨 The Wildcard → unpredictable card selection + outcomes

👀 Social / Group Dynamics
	•	🧑‍⚖️ The Enforcer → plays by the book, no funny business
	•	🤝 The Ally → benefits others / shared effects
	•	🗡️ The Assassin → targets specific players (chaos decisions)
	•	🧍 The Lone Wolf → succeeds while others fail

⸻

🧠 HOW TO ASSIGN THEM (simple logic)

Don’t overcomplicate—pick ONE primary archetype per player.

Suggested priority system:
	1.	Extreme behavior (clear standout → assign immediately)
	2.	Card tendency (risk vs safe vs chaos)
	3.	Performance pattern (clutch, streaky, meltdown)
	4.	Fallback → “Balanced”

⸻

Example rules:
	•	High risk card % + low success → The Gambler
	•	High success rate overall → The Sniper
	•	Success rate spikes late → The Closer
	•	Large point swings hole-to-hole → The Rollercoaster
	•	Most chaos interactions → Chaos Agent
	•	Most missions completed → The Collector

⸻

🎨 MAKE THEM FEEL LIKE A FEATURE (not a label)

Instead of:

“Matt — The Gambler”

Do:

🎲 Matt played like The Gambler
“Went big. Sometimes paid off. Sometimes… very much did not.”

⸻

🪄 PRO MOVE (this is viral potential)

Give archetypes:
	•	Icon
	•	One-liner
	•	Stat backing it up

Example:

🎲 The Gambler
“Full send, every time.”
	•	6 risk cards played
	•	2 completed

⸻

⚠️ WHAT TO AVOID
	•	Too many archetypes per player (1 max, maybe 2 for special rounds)
	•	Overly technical names (“High Variance Performer” = instant buzzkill)
	•	Repetition every round (rotate or weight randomness slightly)
