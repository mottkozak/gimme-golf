These are personal challenge cards with a handicap, restriction, or annoying condition. The reward should usually be a little juicier because the player is being asked to survive nonsense.

Reward: +2

U5. One-Club Down

STYLE CARDS — 15

These are social, funny, theatrical, or mildly ridiculous. Best in party mode, scramble rounds, or when the group is there for vibes and not a U.S. Open qualifier.

T1. Called Shot

Before one shot, call your intended shape or landing zone. Pull it off to score.
Reward: +2

T2. Deadpan

No celebration allowed if you complete your mission. Break character and lose the bonus.
Reward: +1

T3. Finger Guns

After a made putt, celebrate like a complete dork.
Reward: +1

T4. Commentary Mode

Another player narrates your next shot. If you succeed, both earn +1.
Reward: +1 each

T5. Walk-Off Energy

After your final putt, do not watch it. Start walking immediately. It still has to drop.
Reward: +3

T6. Club Twirl Tax

After your best full swing of the hole, you must club twirl. If the group agrees it was worthy, score it.
Reward: +1

T7. PGA Interview

After the hole, explain one shot as if you’re in a post-round interview. Group votes if you committed to the bit.
Reward: +1

T8. Villain Arc

For one shot, openly announce an absurdly aggressive intention. If you pull it off, score it.
Reward: +2

T9. Caddie for a Moment

Let another player choose your club for one shot. If the result is acceptable and you still make bogey or better, score it.
Reward: +2

T10. Respect the Line

Before a putt, dramatically squat and read it like it contains the secrets of the universe. Then make it.
Reward: +2

T11. Signature Move

Declare one shot your “signature shot” before hitting it. If the group agrees it looked pure, score it.
Reward: +1

T12. Broadcaster Voice

Call your own shot in a golf-commentator voice before hitting it. If successful, score it.
Reward: +1

T13. Stone Face Par

Make par or better and react like it meant absolutely nothing.
Reward: +2

T14. Cartoon Confidence

On one putt outside 8 feet, confidently say “that’s in” before the stroke. If it drops, score it.
Reward: +3

T15. Group Approval

Do one ridiculous but harmless celebration after completing your mission. If the group gives it a thumbs-up, score it.
Reward: +1
