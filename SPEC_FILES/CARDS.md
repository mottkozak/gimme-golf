THE FULL 90-CARD DECK

1) COMMON CARDS — 18

These should be achievable enough that everyone stays engaged.

C1. Fairway Finder
Hit the fairway off the tee on a par 4 or 5.
Reward: +1

C2. Green Light
Reach the green in regulation.
Reward: +1

C3. Clean Start
No penalty strokes on the hole.
Reward: +1

C4. Two-Putt Pro
Take no more than two putts once on the green.
Reward: +1

C5. Bogey Saver
Make bogey or better.
Reward: +1

C6. Solid Contact
Advance every full swing at least 70% of the intended distance with no whiffs or duffs.
Reward: +1

C7. No Sand Trouble
Avoid all bunkers on the hole.
Reward: +1

C8. Recovery Artist
Miss the fairway but still make bogey or better.
Reward: +1

C9. Safe Layup
On a par 5, do not go for the green in two, then still make bogey or better.
Reward: +1

C10. First-Putt Friend
Your first putt finishes within 3 feet of the hole.
Reward: +1

C11. Straight Ball
No shot on the hole ends out of bounds or in a hazard.
Reward: +1

C12. Par 3 Survivor
On a par 3, make bogey or better.
Reward: +1

C13. Good Miss
Miss the green but get up-and-down for bogey or better.
Reward: +1

C14. Center Face-ish
No topped or chunked full swings on the hole.
Reward: +1

C15. Keep It Moving
Play the hole without taking a provisional or re-tee.
Reward: +1

C16. Stress-Free Finish
Hole out with one putt from inside 6 feet.
Reward: +1

C17. No Big Number
Score double bogey or better.
Reward: +1

C18. Respectable Hole
Hit either the fairway or the green, and make bogey or better.
Reward: +1

S18. Par 5 Pressure

Public

Public

Public

Public

Public

Public

Public

Public
