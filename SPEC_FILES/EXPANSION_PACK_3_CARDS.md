NOVELTY CARDS — 30

N1. One-Hand Wonder

Hit one shot one-handed during this hole, then finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N2. Opposite-Hand Escape

On one short shot or putt, use your opposite-handed stance and still finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N3. Putter Off the Fringe

From off the green, use putter at least once and still make bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N4. Feet Together

Hit one full or partial shot with your feet together, then finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N5. Knockdown Artist

Declare one shot a low punch or knockdown before hitting it, then pull it off and finish bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N6. Caddie’s Choice

Let another player choose your club for one shot, then finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N7. No Glove, No Problem

Play the entire hole without a glove and finish bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N8. Three-Quarter Specialist

On one full swing, intentionally hit a three-quarter shot and keep it in play. Finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N9. Texas Wedge Life

Use putter from off the green at least once and finish bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N10. Called Bank Shot

Call that your chip or putt will use a slope, bank, or side contour, then execute it well enough to finish within 4 feet or hole it.
Reward: +4

N11. Eyes on the Prize

On one short putt inside 4 feet, hold your finish dramatically until the ball drops. Still make bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N12. Hero Pose

After one shot, hold a ridiculously confident pose until the ball lands. If the shot is acceptable and you finish double bogey or better, score it.
Reward: +3

N13. The Bump-and-Run

Play one intentional bump-and-run chip and get it onto the green or fringe. Finish bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N14. Club Flip Approved

After one clearly good shot, do a club twirl or flip. If the group agrees the shot earned it and you finish double bogey or better, score it.
Reward: +3

N15. Walk-In Putt

Before a putt outside 6 feet, start walking after contact as if it’s going in. If it drops, score it.
Reward: +4

N16. Half-Swing Hustle

Take one intentional half-swing on a recovery or wedge shot and still make bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N17. No Tee, Brave Soul

On one tee shot, hit without using a tee when legal to do so, and keep it in play. Finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N18. Back-Foot Punch

Hit one shot intentionally off the back foot and keep it in play. Finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N19. Putt With Personality

Use an exaggeratedly serious pre-putt routine on one putt, then make it from 5 feet or more.
Reward: +3

N20. The Listener

You must take swing advice from another player on one shot. If you follow it and still finish double bogey or better, score it.
Reward: +4

N21. Flighted Finish

Hit one intentionally flighted wedge or short iron and keep it under control. Finish bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N22. Barely Legal

Use the strangest club in your bag for one reasonable shot and still finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N23. One-Club Wizard

Use the same club for two consecutive non-putt shots and still make bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N24. The Toe Poke

On one recovery shot, intentionally just advance the ball safely instead of trying hero nonsense. Still make bogey or better.
Reward: +3

N25. Declare the Miss

Before an approach, openly declare your intended miss side. If the result matches and you finish bogey or better, score it.
Reward: +3

N26. Lucky Bounce Believer

Before one shot, call for a generous bounce or kick. If the ball gets one and you still finish double bogey or better, score it.
Reward: +3

N27. Soft Hands

On one greenside shot, use your highest-lofted club and leave it on the green. Finish bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N28. No Stepping Off

On one full shot, once you address the ball, you may not step off. Hit it and keep it in play. Finish double bogey or better.
Reward: +4

N29. Trust the Read

Take one putt without a practice stroke and hole it from 6 feet or more.
Reward: +4

N30. Art Project

Attempt one intentionally shaped or creative shot that the group agrees was legitimate. If you pull it off and finish double bogey or better, score it.
Reward: +4
