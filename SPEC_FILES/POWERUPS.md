# Gimme Golf – Power Ups Mode

Power Ups are a standalone game mode in **Gimme Golf**.

In Power Ups Mode, players receive **one random power-up each hole** that allows them to bend the rules of golf in small but entertaining ways.

Power Ups affect **real golf play**, not mission points or card scoring.

## Core Rules

- Each player receives **1 random power-up per hole**
- The power-up may be **used once during the hole**
- The player must **declare it before using it**
- If unused, the power expires at the end of the hole
- Power-ups cannot stack

---

# Putting Powers

## Pinball Wizard
You may tap a **moving putt one additional time** to redirect it toward the hole.  
If it drops, the entire sequence still counts as **one stroke**.

## Backboard
Your playing partners must create a **human backboard** by placing their shoes behind the hole.  
Your putt may bounce off the backboard.

## Gimme Magnet – Short
Any putt **inside 6 feet** automatically counts as holed.

## Gimme Magnet – Medium
Any putt **inside 12 feet** automatically counts as holed.

## Gimme Magnet – Long
Any putt **inside 18 feet** automatically counts as holed.

## One-Putt Wonder
Once your ball reaches the green, the hole can **never require more than one putt**.

## Putt Preview
You may roll **one practice putt that does not count**, then take your real putt.

## The Clutch
If you miss a putt **inside 4 feet**, you may tap it again and it still counts as **one stroke**.

## Laser Line
You may walk the exact **putt line once** with your putter touching the ground.

## Roll Back
If a putt or chip rolls **more than 10 feet past the hole**, you may move the ball **halfway back**.

---

# Lie & Position Powers

## Foot Wedge – Small
Move your ball **1 club length** to improve the lie.

## Foot Wedge – Medium
Move your ball **2 club lengths** to improve the lie.

## Foot Wedge – Deluxe
Move your ball **3 club lengths** to improve the lie.

## Kick Save
If your ball finishes behind a tree, you may move it **1 club length sideways**.

## Fringe Fix
If your ball stops just off the green, you may **place it onto the fringe**.

## Fairway Finder
If your drive lands in rough but in play, you may **move it to the nearest fairway edge**.

## Sand Skip
If your ball lands in a bunker, you may **drop it just outside the bunker**.

## Safety Net
If your shot goes **out of bounds**, drop the ball in bounds with **no penalty stroke**.

---

# Distance Boost Powers

## Power Drive
After hitting your drive, move the ball **1 club length forward**.

## Power Drive – Turbo
After hitting your drive, move the ball **2 club lengths forward**.

## Power Drive – Rocket
After hitting your drive, move the ball **3 club lengths forward**.

## The Booster
After a drive, move your ball **15 yards closer to the hole**.

## Lucky Kick
After a drive, move the ball **one club length toward the green**.

## Tree Friendly
If your ball hits a tree but stays in play, you may move it **10 yards forward**.

## Equalizer
If another player outdrives you by **30+ yards**, move your ball **10 yards forward**.

## Cart Golf
If someone outdrives you on the hole, you may **place your ball at their drive location**.

---

# Hazard & Trouble Powers

## Frozone
If you hit into water, treat the water like **frozen ice**.  
Drop your ball on the **far side of the hazard with no penalty**.

## Ricochet
If your ball kicks **OB from a tree or path**, drop it where it crossed the boundary with **no stroke penalty**.

## Sand Savior
Your **first bunker shot does not count as a stroke** if you escape the bunker.

## Reverse Bounce
If your approach lands on the green but rolls off, you may **place it back on the fringe**.

## Green Light
If your approach rolls off the green, you may **place it back on the edge of the putting surface**.

---

# Shot Replay Powers

## Mulligan Lite
Replay **one shot on the hole**, but you must use the second result.

## Time Warp
Replay your **previous shot**, even if it was good.  
You must use the second result.

---

# Special Shot Powers

## Hand Wedge
Once per hole, instead of hitting the ball with a club, you may **toss the ball with your hand**.

## The Hammer
If you hit the **longest drive on the hole**, subtract **one stroke from your score**.

## High Roller
Before hitting a shot, flip or roll a tee.  
If it lands pointing forward, you may **advance the ball 10 yards after the shot**.

## The Glide
If your approach finishes **within one club length of the green**, you may drop the ball **onto the putting surface**.

## The Walk-Off
If your ball finishes **within 2 feet of the hole**, the putt is automatic.

---

# Recovery Powers

## Long Putt Insurance
If you **three-putt**, remove **one putt** from the score.

## Chip Cushion
If a chip rolls **15 feet past the hole**, move it **halfway back toward the hole**.

## Green Bump
If your chip finishes **within 10 feet**, move it **3 feet closer**.


# Legendary Power Ups

## The Wormhole
Place your ball **anywhere on the green** once per hole.

---

## The Teleporter
Replace **any shot you hit** with a drop **50 yards closer to the hole**.

---

## Gravity Off
Your ball cannot roll **backward away from the hole** on this hole.

---

## Tiger Vision
Once during the hole, you may **place your ball exactly where your shot should have landed**.

---

## The Crowd Goes Wild
All other players must **cheer before your shot**.  
If they do, you may **move your ball 10 yards forward** after the shot.

---

## The Time Loop
Replay **any one shot after seeing the result**.

---

## The Portal
If your ball lands within **20 feet of the green**, you may **place it on the green**.

---

## Ice Rink
All bunker shots on this hole **slide directly onto the green**.

---

## Ball Magnet
If your putt passes within **6 inches of the hole**, it counts as holed.

---

## The Miracle
Once per hole, you may **erase one stroke from your score**.
